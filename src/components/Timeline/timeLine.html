2022

Got a job as a software developer at Acino!

During the early spring / late winter, I contacted Acino, a local IT-consultant firm, and was offered to become a student ambassador for them. My job was basically being a link between Acino and students, and organizing code-nights, where students could come and talk and learn about programming.

This eventually led to my first job within the field. During the summer of 2022, I work as a software developer for Acino AB.
